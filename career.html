Opportunities at AMG Security

AMG Security proudly offers state-of-the-art CCTV Monitoring services across England, providing an essential layer of security for both businesses and residences. Our approach is built on utilizing the latest in surveillance technology to offer real-time, continuous monitoring of your property.

  • Career Growth and Development

    We offer careers in private security in various aspects of security and surveillance. Our security guards have the opportunity to work on diverse projects, gaining experience in multiple facets of the security industry. We believe in promoting from within, providing ample opportunities for advancement to leadership positions.

  • Training and Certification

    At AMG Security, we ensure that our security professionals are equipped with the latest skills and knowledge in the field. We offer regular training sessions, workshops, and seminars to keep our team updated on the newest security technologies and practices. Additionally, we assist our employees in acquiring essential certifications and licenses, enhancing their professional qualifications.

  • Diverse and Inclusive Work Environment

    We pride ourselves on fostering an inclusive culture that values diversity. Our team comprises individuals from various backgrounds, each bringing unique perspectives and ideas to the table. We believe that a diverse workforce is key to innovative and effective security solutions.

Working with AMG Security

AMG security team offers job opportunities that prioritize a supportive and respectful workplace environment. We value the contribution of each employee and encourage open communication and collaboration. Safety, integrity, and professionalism are at the core of our operations, and we expect our team members to uphold these values in their work.

You can expect to be part of a team that is dedicated to making a difference, providing security and peace of mind to our clients. We offer competitive salaries, full-time security job, official employment in the security field, comprehensive benefits, and a work-life balance that respects your personal time and commitments.
