About the service

AMG Security, a premier security service provider in England, specializes in offering comprehensive events security services. Understanding the critical importance of safety at events, our service is designed to ensure that every gathering, big or small, proceeds without any security hitches. Our company works with various event sizes. We provide VIP event protection, public event security, sporting event security, wedding event protection, exhibition security services and much more.

  • Tailored Security Plans

    We understand that each property is unique and requires a personalized security strategy. Our specialized team conducts a thorough property risk assessment to tailor a unique security plan for your real estate. This involves identifying vulnerabilities specific to your property and evaluating potential risks.

  • Comprehensive Coverage

    We deploy cutting-edge property surveillance systems like high-definition cameras, motion sensors, and real estate alarm systems. These technologies seamlessly integrate into a unified security setup managed by our skilled team. Additionally, our solutions encompass CCTV specifically designed for real estate security.

  • Expert Team and Advanced Technology

    Our team consists of highly qualified and experienced professionals who specialise in providing security for events of any scale, from high-profile event security (red carpet event security, celebrity event protection) to concert security, festival safety, corporate event security or private party security. They are supported by the latest security technologies, including event crowd control services, surveillance equipment, and emergency response protocols.

Stages of work

  • 01

    Initial Consultation:

    Contact us to discuss your event and security requirements. Share the details and objectives of your event to help us understand your needs.

  • 02

    Event Assessment:

    We conduct a detailed evaluation of your event's security needs, considering factors like size, location, and audience.

  • 03

    Custom Security Plan Development:

    Based on the assessment, we craft a personalized security strategy that caters to the specific needs of your event.

  • 04

    Security Team Briefing and Training:

    Prior to the event, our security team is thoroughly briefed and trained on the event-specific security plan and protocols.

  • 05

    Implementation:

    Our team is deployed to manage and oversee all security aspects of your event, ensuring every detail is covered.

  • 06

    Post-Event Review:

    Following the event, we provide a comprehensive review and feedback session to evaluate the security measures and discuss any improvements for future events
