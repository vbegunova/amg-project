About the service

AMG Security leads the industry in England, delivering top-tier Personal Protection services. We specialize in offering personalized security measures to precisely match the distinct needs of every individual client, specifically focusing on personal safety measures.

    Experience of our expert bodyguards extends to conducting personal threat assessments, providing private bodyguard services, delivering high-profile individual protection, еxecutive protection, ensuring exclusive personal protection, and offering confidential security services. Our bodyguards for hire adeptness in navigating various environments guarantees your safety without disrupting your daily activities.

  • Customized Security Approach

    We understand that personal safety and security is a highly individualized service. Our approach begins with a thorough understanding of your specific security needs, lifestyle, and preferences. We then develop a tailored individual safety solutions that offers maximum security while maintaining your comfort and privacy.

  • Advanced Risk Assessment

    Prior to assignment, our team conducts a comprehensive risk assessment during the personal security consulting. This includes evaluating potential threats, identifying vulnerabilities, and preparing contingency plans to ensure a proactive approach to your safety.

    At AMG Security, our emphasis lies in maintaining discretion and professionalism. Our officers undergo training to seamlessly integrate into your environment, ensuring professional security for individuals that is both effective and inconspicuous.
